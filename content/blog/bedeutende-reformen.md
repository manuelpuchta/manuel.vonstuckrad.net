I am speechless and paralysed by this political incompetence. Here we go [again](./germany-slips-back-into-fascism.md).

_Rage incoming_

The "[bedeutenden Reformen](https://www.bundesregierung.de/breg-de/aktuelles/reformen-rente-arbeitsmarkt-steuern-2445598)" that have just been unveiled by our government in the last couple of days will once again lead to right-wing parties gaining further ground in the polls. The reforms in particular:

- Grundsicherung, an even stricter version of the previous Bürgergeld.
- From now on, a doctor’s certificate will be required from the very first day of illness, if the employer follows that decission. It’ll be a nightmare if you actually do need to see a doctor.
- Preventing "Verstaatlichung privater Wohnungsbaugesellschaften", for example [Deutsche Wohnen & Co enteignen](https://dwenteignen.de/).
- Expansion of unjustified fixed-term employment contracts. It's already so hard for many people to find a secure job.
- And, another crazy one: planning to abolish the Freedom of Information Act (Informationsfreiheitsgesetz) soonish.

How can anyone be so stupid as to not finally make a change in direction to make life better and more affordable for everyone?

With the current policies of the Union and the SPD, they will only dig themselves deeper into a hole. Into a grave 🪦. Fascism is near. Undemocratic times are ahead of us. Winter is coming.

I am now convinced that, without proceedings to ban the AfD, there will be no way to prevent its success in the upcoming Bundestag election.

It's so incredibly frustrating. But not really surprising to me. When people with Porsches and private jets decide on reforms, those without privileges are left behind.
