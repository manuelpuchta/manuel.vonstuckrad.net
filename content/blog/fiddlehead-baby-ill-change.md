It took me a while to really warm up with Pat Flynn’s music. I have no idea why. Since around 2020, I’ve been happily listening to [Have Heart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Have_Heart) on repeat. “Songs To Scream At The Sun” is amazing. I only really got into [Fiddlehead](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fiddlehead_(band)>) this year - or rather, my ears are craving more and more of it. The current pre-release for the upcoming “Baby I’ll Change” album (EP) is fantastic. Excessive consumption isn't entirely foreign to me, though thankfully alcohol (almost) and cigarettes are now a thing of the past for me. Still, I can really relate to the song because of acquaintances and old friends and general Weltschmerz. Thanks, Mr. Flynn.
